TM Pro

A TM PRO package grants your team access to a lot of different features in the game. Some will make your team look more stylish, some will give you extra insight in your players with more statistics and game analysis, and finally some will make your life easier while playing Trophy Manager.

We currently have 8000 teams with a TM PRO package. When you buy a TM PRO package you will have access to a huge amount of extra features for the number of days you buy. If you have any days left your new purchase will be added to the already existing days.

Transfer Agent

Get a transfer agent to pursue a player and make the bids for you with predefined instructions

Squad Filters

Have a large squad on need make the player list shorter and easier? Use the squad filters to filter by age, position, and A- or B-team.

Shortlist Filters

Use filters to manage your giant shortlist. See player stats, skills or filter from positions and age.

Team Analyzer

Get detailed insight about your team and your opponents. You will find breakdowns of everything from player performance, tactical insight, finishing types, defensive actions and much much more in the team analyzer tool.

Transfer Monitor

The transfer monitor searches the transferlist for you and reports back if there are any players of your interest for sale
